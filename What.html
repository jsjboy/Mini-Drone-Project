What definally is a Mini Drone?

Sustainable mini drone are made with recyclable and biodegradable materials.
This drone will use long-lasting batteries and efficient systems to reduce energy consumption.

Why do it by this way?

Because the process of designing and building it allows us to explore innovative solutions while also addressing environmental issues.
That can reduce significally the ambiental damage.
